Find answers to common questions about CoursGen

General

What is CoursGen?
CoursGen is a comprehensive online learning platform that provides tools for creating, managing, and delivering online courses.
Who can use CoursGen?
CoursGen is suitable for educators, trainers, businesses, and organizations looking to create and deliver online courses.

Pricing

How much does CoursGen cost?
We offer various pricing plans to suit different needs. Please visit our Pricing page for detailed information.
Is there a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all our plans. No credit card is required to start your trial.

Features

Can I create quizzes and assessments?
Yes, CoursGen provides tools to create various types of quizzes, tests, and assessments to evaluate student learning.
Does CoursGen support video content?
Absolutely! You can easily upload and embed video content in your courses, as well as host live webinars.

Technical

Is CoursGen mobile-friendly?
Yes, CoursGen is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
How secure is my data?
We take security seriously. All data is encrypted, and we use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.
